## Contribution Categories

Contributors are recognized in these categories:

### Neuro-Rights Advocacy
Individuals who have advanced the legal and ethical frameworks for cognitive sovereignty.

### Technical Implementation
Developers who have built crates, ALN schemas, and enforcement mechanisms.

### Documentation
Writers who have made the project accessible through clear documentation and tutorials.

### Research
Researchers who have analyzed threats, historical context, and emerging frameworks.

### Translation
Volunteers who have translated core documents into additional languages.
